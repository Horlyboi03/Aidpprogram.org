AIDP is designed for members that are suffering from diseases, health issues, cancer, disabled, poor, widowed, widow, divorced, surgery, retired, married, single, young, old, semi retired, self employed and many other issues.

The program, in conjunction with the Private Grant Foundation, will issue billions of dollars in grants to a variety of groups each year. Grants are awarded to individuals every day.

Grant Programs Are Not Loans

You decide how much you need, as long as the amount is legal and meets the requirements of foundations and government agencies. The money is yours and never needs to be repaid.

Tax-Free & Interest-Free

Grant money is not taxable and bears no interest. You keep 100% of what you receive.

No Credit Checks Required

Grant programs don't require a credit check, a security deposit, or a cosigner. You can apply even if you have bad credit.

Your Right as a Taxpayer

You, as a taxpayer, have the right to apply for this money. This program is designed to help pay medical bills, buy a home, be a seller, start a business, go to school, retire, and much more.

The program is worldwide. Grants are awarded to individuals every day.
