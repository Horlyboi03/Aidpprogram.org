═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  HOW TO DELETE CONVERSATIONS NOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MOBILE (Touch):
1. Touch and hold conversation
2. Swipe left
3. Red "DELETE" button appears from right
4. Tap delete button
5. Confirmation modal shows
6. Confirm deletion

DESKTOP (Mouse):
1. Click and hold conversation
2. Drag left
3. Red "DELETE" button appears from right
4. Click delete button
5. Confirmation modal shows
6. Confirm deletion

CANCEL:
→ Swipe/drag right to hide delete button
→ Click elsewhere
→ Press Escape in modal
